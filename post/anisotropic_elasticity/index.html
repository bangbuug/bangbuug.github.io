Anisotropic elasticity（各向异性弹性）

由胡克定律正交坐标下的张量表达：

σij=Cijklεkl\sigma _ { i j } = C _ { i j k l } \varepsilon _ { k l } σij​=Cijkl​εkl​

由对称性：

Cijkt=Cjikl,Cijkl=Cijlk,Cijkl=CklijC_{i j} k t=C_{j i k l}, \quad C_{i j} k l=C_{i j} l k, \quad C_{i j} k l=C_{k l i j} Cij​kt=Cjikl​,Cij​kl=Cij​lk,Cij​kl=Cklij​

Orthotropic elasticity(正交各向异性弹性)

正交各向异性是指由三个弹性对称面的材料，如纤维增强材料，木材等

由对称性可知

{σx=c11εx+c12εy+c13εzτyz=c44γyzσy=c21εx+c22εy+c23εzτzx=c55γzxσz=c31εx+c32εy+c33εzτxy=c66γxy\left\{\begin{array}{l}{\sigma_{x}=c_{11} \varepsilon_{x}+c_{12} \varepsilon_{y}+c_{13} \varepsilon_{z} \quad \tau _{yz}=c_{44} \gamma_{yz}} \\ {\sigma_{y}=c_{21} \varepsilon_{x}+c_{22} \varepsilon_{y}+c_{23} \varepsilon_{z} \quad \tau_{zx}=c_{55} \gamma_{zx}} \\ {\sigma_{z}=c_{31} \varepsilon_{x}+c_{32} \varepsilon_{y}+c_{33} \varepsilon_{z} \quad \tau _{x y} = c_{66} \gamma_{x y}}\end{array}\right. ⎩⎨⎧​σx​=c11​εx​+c12​εy​+c13​εz​τyz​=c44​γyz​σy​=c21​εx​+c22​εy​+c23​εz​τzx​=c55​γzx​σz​=c31​εx​+c32​εy​+c33​εz​τxy​=c66​γxy​​

平面应变条件下的Orthotropic elasticity

柔度方程

\left\{\begin{array}{l}{\varepsilon_{1}=\frac{1}{E_{1}} \sigma_{1}-\frac{\nu_{21}}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}-\frac{\nu_{31}}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3}} \\ {\varepsilon_{2}=-\frac{\nu_{2}}{E_{1}} \sigma_{1}+\frac{1}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}-\frac{\nu_{32}}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3}} \\ {\varepsilon_{3}=-\frac{\nu_{13}}{E_{1} }\sigma_{1}-\frac{\nu_{23}}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}+\frac{1}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3}\\\gamma_{23}=\frac{1}{G_{13}} \tau_{23}} \\ {\gamma_{31}=\frac{1}{G_{31}} \tau_{31}} \\ {\gamma_{12}=\frac{1}{G_{12}} \tau_{12}}\end{array}\right.

由平面应变条件

σ3=γ23=γ31\sigma_{3} = \gamma_{23}=\gamma_{31} σ3​=γ23​=γ31​

带入柔度方程，由于在平面应变条件下，仅考虑xOy平面的变形，因此柔度方程可以简化为：

[εxxεyy2εxy]=[1−ν31ν13E1−ν21+ν31ν23E20−ν12+ν32ν23E11−ν32ν23E20001G12][σxxσyyσxy]\left[\begin{array}{c}{\varepsilon_{x x}} \\ {\varepsilon_{y y}} \\ {2\varepsilon_{x y}}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}{\frac{1-\nu_{31}\nu_{13}}{E_{1}}} & {-\frac{\nu_{21}+\nu_{31}\nu_{23}}{E_{2}}} & {0} \\ {-\frac{\nu_{12}+\nu_{32}\nu_{23}}{E_{1}}} & {\frac{1-\nu_{32} \nu_{23}}{E_{2}}} & {0} \\ {0} & {0} & {\frac{1}{G_{12}}}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}{\sigma_{x x}} \\ {\sigma_{y y}} \\ {\sigma_{x y}}\end{array}\right] ⎣⎡​εxx​εyy​2εxy​​⎦⎤​=⎣⎡​E1​1−ν31​ν13​​−E1​ν12​+ν32​ν23​​0​−E2​ν21​+ν31​ν23​​E2​1−ν32​ν23​​0​00G12​1​​⎦⎤​⎣⎡​σxx​σyy​σxy​​⎦⎤​

则独立的弹性常数可以缩减到7个：E1⋅E2,E3,ν12,ν23,ν31,G12E_{1} \cdot E_{2}, E_{3}, \nu_{12}, \nu_{23}, \nu_{31}, G_{12}E1​⋅E2​,E3​,ν12​,ν23​,ν31​,G12​

若考虑incompressibility（不可压缩性）（详见后），独立弹性常数可以进一步缩减到4个：E1⋅E2,E3,G12E_{1} \cdot E_{2}, E_{3}, G_{12}E1​⋅E2​,E3​,G12​

平面应力条件下的Orthotropic elasticity

柔度方程

\left\{\begin{array}{l}{\varepsilon_{1}=\frac{1}{E_{1}} \sigma_{1}-\frac{\nu_{21}}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}-\frac{\nu_{31}}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3}} \\ {\varepsilon_{2}=-\frac{\nu_{2}}{E_{1}} \sigma_{1}+\frac{1}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}-\frac{\nu_{32}}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3}} \\ {\varepsilon_{3}=-\frac{\nu_{13}}{E_{1} }\sigma_{1}-\frac{\nu_{23}}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}+\frac{1}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3}\\\gamma_{23}=\frac{1}{G_{13}} \tau_{23}} \\ {\gamma_{31}=\frac{1}{G_{31}} \tau_{31}} \\ {\gamma_{12}=\frac{1}{G_{12}} \tau_{12}}\end{array}\right.

由平面应变条件：

ε3=γ23=γ31=0\varepsilon_{3} = \gamma_{23} = \gamma_{31} = 0 ε3​=γ23​=γ31​=0

带入柔度方程，由于在平面应力条件下，仅考虑xOy平面的变形，因此柔度方程可以简化为：

[εxxεyy2εxy]=[1E1−ν21E20−ν12E11E20001G12][σxxσyyσxy]\left[\begin{array}{l}{\varepsilon_{x x}} \\ {\varepsilon_{y y}} \\ {2\varepsilon_{xy} }\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}{\frac{1}{E_{1}}} & {-\frac{\nu_{21}}{E_{2}}} & {0} \\ {-\frac{\nu_{12}}{E_{1}}} & {\frac{1}{E_{2}}} & {0} \\ {0} & {0} & {\frac{1}{G_{12}}}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}{\sigma_{x x}} \\ {\sigma_{y y}} \\ {\sigma_{x y}}\end{array}\right] ⎣⎡​εxx​εyy​2εxy​​⎦⎤​=⎣⎡​E1​1​−E1​ν12​​0​−E2​ν21​​E2​1​0​00G12​1​​⎦⎤​⎣⎡​σxx​σyy​σxy​​⎦⎤​

则独立的弹性常数可以缩减为4个，分别为E1⋅E2,ν12,G12E_{1} \cdot E_{2},\nu_{12}, G_{12}E1​⋅E2​,ν12​,G12​

若考虑incompressibility（不可压缩性），独立弹性常数仍为4个：E1⋅E2,E3,G12E_{1} \cdot E_{2}, E_{3}, G_{12}E1​⋅E2​,E3​,G12​

incompressibility（不可压缩性）

不可压缩性，意为着体积应变为0：

θ=ε11+ε22+ε33=1−ν12−ν13E1σ1+1−ν21−ν23E2σ2+1−ν31−ν32E3σ3=0\begin{aligned} \theta &=\varepsilon_{11}+\varepsilon_{22}+\varepsilon_{33} \\ &=\frac{1-\nu_{12}-\nu_{13}}{E_{1}} \sigma_{1}+\frac{1-\nu_{21}-\nu_{23}}{E_{2}} \sigma_{2}+\frac{1-\nu_{31}-\nu_{32}}{E_{3}} \sigma_{3} \\ &=0 \end{aligned} θ​=ε11​+ε22​+ε33​=E1​1−ν12​−ν13​​σ1​+E2​1−ν21​−ν23​​σ2​+E3​1−ν31​−ν32​​σ3​=0​

可以推得弹性参数间需要满足以下关系：

νij=12Ej(1Ei+1Ej−1Ek)(k≠i≠j,i,j,k=1,2,3)\nu_{i j}=\frac{1}{2} E_{j}\left(\frac{1}{E_{i}}+\frac{1}{E_{j}}-\frac{1}{E_{k}}\right)\quad (k \neq i \neq j, i, j, k=1,2,3) νij​=21​Ej​(Ei​1​+Ej​1​−Ek​1​)(k​=i​=j,i,j,k=1,2,3)

因此，只需要定义E和G就可以了。
